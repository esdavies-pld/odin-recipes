Description

This lasagna recipe is an old family secret...until now.

My Nonna used to make this back in Sicily, then again once we moved to central Jersey.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 3/4 pound Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna Noodles/Cheese Topping

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

Prep Work

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside.

Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil and add diced onions
  2. Add the ground beef and sausage, increasing heat to medium high. Cook through.
  3. Add chicken borth, marinara, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to boil and reduce to a simmer.

Preheat oven and cook noodles

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Boil large pot of salted pasta water
  3. Once boiling, add noodle and cook to al dente.
  4. Drain and rinse with cold water.
